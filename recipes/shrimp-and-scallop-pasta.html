Shrimp and Scallop Pasta

Description

This is a simple dish consisting of fresh scallops and shrimp with linguinni or fettucini pasta and an alfredo sauce.

The deeply seared scallops lend a beautiful flavor to the sauce and really sets this apart from other basic alfredo pasta dishes.

INGREDIENTS

  • 5ea - Scallops, large
  • 4oz - Shrimp, raw
  • 1oz - Parmesan Cheese, grated
  • 8oz - Heavy Cream
  • 2oz - White Wine
  • 1tsp - Black Pepper
  • 4oz - Fetuccini, dry
  • 2Tbps - Fresh Parsley, chopped
