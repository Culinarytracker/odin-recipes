Shrimp and Scallop Pasta

Description

This is a simple dish consisting of fresh scallops and shrimp with linguinni or fettucini pasta and an alfredo sauce.

The deeply seared scallops lend a beautiful flavor to the sauce and really sets this apart from other basic alfredo pasta dishes.

INGREDIENTS

  • 5ea - Scallops, large
  • 4oz - Shrimp, raw
  • 1oz - Parmesan Cheese, grated
  • 8oz - Heavy Cream
  • 2oz - White Wine
  • 1tsp - Black Pepper
  • 4oz - Fettuccine, dry
  • 2Tbps - Fresh Parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat a large pan and sear the scallops until dark in color. Flip scallops and add shrimp. Cook until the other side of the scallops are deeply browned.

  2. Deglaze pan with white wine and reduce until almost dry.

  3. Add heavy cream and simmer until color has darkened, then add parmesan cheese and black pepper.

  4. Add cooked pasta and toss with half of the parsley. Serve in a large bowl and garnish with remaining parsley.
