Salmon with Lemon and Capers

DESCRIPTION

A quick and easy Salmon dish with a zangy lemon and caper butter sauce.

This salmon can be made either sauteed or grilled, but the sauce works better when sauteed in my option because you get to deglaze the salmon fond from the pan. I prefer to let the capers fry up a bit before deglazing with the lemon juice. Also the butter should be added so there is just enough heat to get it all melted and well emulsified.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2ea - Salmon Filets
  • 2oz - lemon juice, fresh
  • 1Tbsp - Capers with brine
  • 1/4 cup - Butter, cold diced
  • garnish - Parsley, fresh

STEPS

  1. Cook the salmon until just done. Remove from the pan.

  2. Add capers and let fry in the oil for about a minute.

  3. Deglaze with lemon juice and reduce by half. Remove from heat.

  4. Add half of the diced butter and swirl pan to combine. Add remaining butter as it melts.

  5. Spoon sauce over salmon and garnish with fresh parsley.
