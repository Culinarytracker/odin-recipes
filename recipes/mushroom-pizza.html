DESCRIPTION

Mushroom Pizza

A simple mushroom pizza with mozzarella and fetta cheeses.

INGREDIENTS

  • 8oz - Pizza Dough
  • 1 cup - Mushrooms, sliced
  • 2oz - Pizza Sauce
  • 2oz - Crumbled Fetta Cheese
  • 2Tbsp - Chives, sliced

STEPS

  1. Roll out dough to a 12" circle.

  2. Spread sauce onto dough evenly, leaving a small gap at the edge.

  3. Place mushroom slices evenly over the surface of the dough.

  4. Top with cheese and cook at 500 until done.
